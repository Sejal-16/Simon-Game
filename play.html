How To Play ?

1. Press any key to start the game.
2. Any random color will be selected by computer ,and its corresponding sound will be played.
3. Player will have to hit that particular block only , remembering the previous sequence also.
4. In the first level , computer will play say red. player has to hit red. Then player is promoted to next level and comp plays yellow. But now player has to hit red as well as yellow. If player succeeds he will reach level 3 and so on.
5. If the sequence is not correctly identified , then game will be over.
